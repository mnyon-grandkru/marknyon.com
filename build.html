Built for EBWiki


I am one of the founders of EBWiki. In discussions with other members of the "Blacks in Technology" group, a global platform for black women and men in technology, we came up with the idea of keeping track of all cases of black people killed by police, because in mainstream culture, we have observed that many people perceive that police don't kill black people as often as they do.

I helped build the initial technology platform and have been workingwith my teammates since 2015 to continuously gather and update the data so that it can be useful in ending the violence against people of color.

Built for Queer Global


Queer Global - As one of the volunteer projects I work upon, I help QueerGlobal make decisions on how to proceed. I also help set up development practices for the tech department as well as mentor people working on individual projects and tasks. Finally, I'm help QueerGlobal develop a roadmap and define the value the department brings to QueerGlobal.

Built for The Lamp


The Lamp - Board of Directors - I served on the board of this nonprofit that was dedicated to providing training to help people be better evaluating media and not being taken in by misinformation. This nonprofit had programs in several grade schools in the NYC area. We made recommendations for the program budget as well set the direction of how the non-profit would expand.

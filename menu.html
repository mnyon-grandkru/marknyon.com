Mark Nyon is a technologist and entrepreneur who has been developing Web applications since 1996 when he graduated from Tufts University.

He has worked at consulting agencies, enterprise companies, startups and businesses of all sizes.

After a five year stint at MTV Networks, in 2006 he started working independently and full-time for Grand Kru Technologies as a consultant, then later returned to work as a full-time employee.

Mark has developed and architected web sites and applications, managed teams and improved software development processes for small web incubators to large enterprise companies. He has worked in many industries including education, fashion, financial services, healthcare and media.

His current focus is on education, DEI (Diversity, Equity & Inclusion) and other aspects of technology. He is currently working as a Senior Software Engineer at 2U.

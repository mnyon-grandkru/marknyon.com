EBWiki Co-Founder
Co-Founder and on the Board of Directors. The site was created to provide victims, their families and their communities with the tools to end the abuse and indiscriminate killing by the police of African Americans and other people of color.

2U - Senior Software Engineer
Added multiple features to the 2U Gradebook application, including internationalization and LTI (Learning Tools Interoperability) compliance. Reviewed code, improved documentation & institutional knowledge. Worked on systems to integrate internal systems into Zoom classrooms. Interviewed prospective hires through pair programming exercises as well as panel reviews. Global Communications Chair for BNET, the Business Resource Network for African-Americans.
